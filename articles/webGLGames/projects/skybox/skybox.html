Interactable Lighting

Originally, this demo started out as me learning about adding a sky box to a scene so future demos are more visually interesting. I added the monkey model from Blender and had the camera automatically spinning around the mesh before realizing that it would be nicer to control the camera yourself. Then, I wanted to see if I could make the shader dynamic and added more controls for the lighting.

Overall, I'm happier with the demo being interactable. It's more fun than just having the camera spin around the monkey model.
