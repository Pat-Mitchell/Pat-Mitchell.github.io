Interactable Lighting

Originally, this demo started out as me learning about adding a sky box to a scene so future demos are more visually interesting. I added the monkey model from Blender and had the camera automatically spinning around the mesh before realizing that it would be nicer to control the camera yourself. Then, I felt that one control was too barron, so I added more for the lighting and color.

Overall, I'm happier with the demo being interactable. It's more fun than just having the camera spin around the monkey model.
