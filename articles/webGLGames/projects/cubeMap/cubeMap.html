Cube mapped mesh

This project was inspired by just finding the cubeSampler built-in function in OpenGL.

Six individual textures are mapped to the different faces of the cube and then basic lighting is added to the scene.

Another improvement made is that this cube mesh is smooth shaded insted of flat shaded like in previous demos. The default setting in blender is flat shading and my limited experience with the software lead me to export meshes with surface normals instead of vertex normals.
